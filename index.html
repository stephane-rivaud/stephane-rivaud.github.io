Stéphane Rivaud

Postdoctoral Researcher · INRIA Saclay · TAU Team

Email Google Scholar GitHub CV

I develop functional optimization methods for neural architecture growth and decentralized machine learning for hardware acceleration. My work focuses on efficient training methods, generative modeling, and integrating domain knowledge into deep learning systems.
